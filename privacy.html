What Data Do We Collect (and Where)?

This is the most important part: This app stores ALL your data locally in your browser's storage. Nothing is sent to a server. Your data, your device. Simple as that.

Cat Trucker Profile Data

When you create a profile, we store your name, nickname, specialty, etc. This is stored ONLY on your device so you can see your trucker profile.

Time and Route Data

We store the routes you create and the time entries you log. This is the core function of the app - your digital logbook, stored safely on your own device!

Your Browser Storage is Your Database!

We use your browser's local storage to save everything. This is like your cat having its own secret stash of toys under the sofa. It's yours, it's private, and no one else can see it.

A Big Cat Warning About Your Data

Since all data is stored locally, it is YOUR responsibility to back it up using the 'Time Trucking Backup' feature. If you clear your browser's cache, cookies, or site data, your trucking logs WILL be permanently deleted. Pringles cannot get them back for you. He will just give you a grumpy look. You have been warned (again) by a cat.

Your Rights as a User

You have full control over your data because it's on your device:

  • Right to access your data (it's right here in the app).
  • Right to correct your data (just edit it).
  • Right to request deletion (just delete your profile or clear your browser data, but be warned!).
  • Right to data portability (use the 'Export' function!).
